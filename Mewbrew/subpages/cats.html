Adoption Process

Spend time with the cats at our café to see who you connect with. Our staff can help introduce you to cats that match your lifestyle.

Fill out our adoption inquiry form below or ask staff for a paper application. We'll review it within 2-3 business days.

We conduct a virtual home visit to ensure a safe environment for your new family member. This usually takes 15-20 minutes.

Complete the paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and bring your new friend home! We'll provide a starter kit with food and supplies.
